Okay, this game is all about the webbed relationship between actors and the movies they act in. The better you know your favorite movies, their casts, and the other movies those actors have appeared in, the better you'll do! How it works is you start with an actor, Morgan Freeman for example, then name a movie he has acted in. Let's go with cult-classic and Oscar-sweeping "Evan Almighty" which opens the door to Steve Carell, who was also in that movie. From Steve Carell, "The 40 Year-Old Virgin" would be a great option, as tons of well known actors such as Seth Rogen, Paul Rudd, Leslie Mann, and Jane Lynch are now playable.


Based on the Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon, this Beat-The-Streak style game relies on the bipartite graph created by actors and movies. More game-modes, features, and insights based on how you play the game are coming soon!
